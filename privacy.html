Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Welcome to Papaya Peg. This privacy policy explains how Matthew Fadoul ("I", "me", or "the Developer") handles data within the Papaya Peg iOS application.

1. Overview

Papaya Peg is designed to be a privacy-focused game. I do not require user accounts, and I do not collect or store your personal identity, name, or contact information on any servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

What is stored: Papaya Peg stores game state, high scores, favorites, and settings locally on your device using standard iOS storage. I do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device unless Apple's iCloud sync is active on your account, in which case Apple may sync it to your other devices through your personal Apple ID — I have no access to that synced data either.

Specifically, the app stores:

  • Current and saved game state for each puzzle variant
  • High score records
  • Favorited games
  • App preferences (e.g. haptic feedback setting)

No data is transmitted to any server operated by me or any third party. The app contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking.

3. Advertising

Papaya Peg contains no advertising. No ad SDKs are included in the app, and no advertising data is collected or transmitted.

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Papaya Peg does not request ATT permission and does not track your activity across other apps or websites.

5. Children's Privacy

Papaya Peg does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age. The app is suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If future versions of the app introduce new features that affect data handling, this policy will be updated before or alongside that release.
